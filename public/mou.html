This Memorandum of Understanding ("MOU") is entered into between Quantech Infosystem LLP. (hereinafter "the Provider") and the Institution (hereinafter "the School"), collectively referred to as "the Parties."

3. Obligations of the Provider

  • Provide full access to the Quantech Platform modules as agreed, including Academics, Fee Management, Attendance, Reports, Hostel, and Transport (as applicable).
  • Ensure 99.5% platform uptime during school operational hours.
  • Provide onboarding support, staff training sessions (online/on-site), and technical documentation.
  • Maintain data confidentiality and comply with applicable data protection laws.
  • Deliver feature updates and security patches throughout the agreement period at no additional cost.

4. Obligations of the School

  • Appoint a designated Quantech Platform Coordinator responsible for internal rollout and communication.
  • Provide accurate and complete student data for onboarding within 14 days of agreement execution.
  • Ensure timely payment of subscription fees, with 70% of commercials to be paid upfront upon invoice issuance by the Provider.
  • Not share, sub-license, or resell access to the Quantech Platform to any third party.
  • Report technical issues through the designated support channel promptly.

5. Confidentiality

Both Parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality of all information exchanged under this MOU, including student data, pricing, and platform configurations. Student data shall be used solely for the purpose of providing the agreed services and shall not be shared with any third party without prior written consent.

6. Duration & Termination

This MOU shall be effective from the date of signing and shall remain valid for a period of one (1) academic year, renewable annually by mutual written consent. Either Party may terminate this MOU with 30 days' written notice. Upon termination, the School's data shall be made available for export for a period of 30 days before deletion.

7. Limitation of Liability

The Provider's total liability under this MOU shall not exceed the total fees paid by the School in the preceding 3 months. The Provider shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from the use or inability to use the platform.

8. Governing Law

This MOU shall be governed by the laws of India. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this MOU shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts in Dhule, Maharashtra.

9. Entire Agreement

This MOU constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions, representations, or agreements. Amendments to this MOU shall be valid only if made in writing and signed by both Parties.
